A YOUNG English professional footballer hoping to play for England one day has had to move to Romania to progress his career.
Striker Wilson Kneeshaw, 19, from Middlesbrough, has just signed a two-year contract with ACS Poli Timisoara.
His move turns the current UK immigration issue on its head as he struggles to fulfil his ambitions at home.
