FRENCH voters went to the polls in council elections yesterday as experts predicted major gains for the far-right National Front.
Marine Le Pen's anti-immigration party was expected to gain control of several cities and towns.
The centre-right were also expected to do well at the expense of the governing socialists, who are under fire over the performance of the French economy.
