IMMIGRANTS have cost British taxpayers more than £22million a day for 17 years, a report claims.
Research by Migration Watch UK says foreigners arriving here cost £140billion between 1995 and 2011. However, other researchers, who previously claimed immigrants contributed 34 per cent more in taxes than they received in benefits, accused the authors of 'fundamental mistakes'.
