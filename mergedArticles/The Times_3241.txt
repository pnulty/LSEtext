Further restrictions are to be placed on the access that EU migrants have to benefits, in an attempt by ministers to stem public concerns about immigration.
EU workers coming to Britain have to wait three months before receiving unemployment benefit under measures introduced in January. However, the Treasury has now ruled that they should wait three months before being eligible for child benefit and child tax credits. The ruling comes before next month's European elections, when the Tories face a strong challenge from Ukip.
