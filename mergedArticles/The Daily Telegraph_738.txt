MONDAY, 7 APRIL
Commons: Communities and Local Government Questions. A debate on justice and home affairs. A short debate on the proposed closure of Worksop Magistrates Court.
Westminster Hall: Not sitting.
Lords: Questions. Immigration Bill, Report stage (Day 3). A debate on the balance between regular and reserve forces in the Armed Forces.
TUESDAY, 8 APRIL
Commons: Foreign and Commonwealth Office Questions. Ten Minute Rule Motion: National Health Service (Right to Treatment). Finance (No 2) Bill, Committee of the whole House. A short debate on transparency and public trust in business.
Westminster Hall: Debates on: Broadband in the North of England; Anti–social behaviour and renting of houses for stag and hen parties; Military credit union; Fairness in pension provision for contributors; Business lending.
Lords: Questions. Pensions Bill, Consideration of Commons amendments. Water Bill, Third reading.
