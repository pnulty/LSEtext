1999 JOE DiMaggio died 15 years ago today, leaving a nation in mourning. He was 84. The Yankees baseball legend had a 56-game hitting streak which the New York Times called "perhaps the most enduring record in sport". The son of poor Italian fishing immigrants, he is certainly the only man to have married Marilyn Monroe and be namedropped in Simon & Garfunkel's Mrs Robinson.
