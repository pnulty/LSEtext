INSIDE POLITICS
TORY Chief Whip Sir George Young herded backbenchers into a supportive huddle around Culture Secretary Maria Miller moments before her perfunctory apology to the Commons on Thursday for excessive expenses claims. But former immigration minister Mark Harper shook his head and declined to join in. Involved in his own scandal over mistakenly employing an illegal immigrant earlier this year Mr Harper did the decent thing and offered his resignation rather than try to cling on to his job.
