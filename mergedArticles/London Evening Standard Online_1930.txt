Man and woman due in court accused of holding someone in slavery or servitude

A man and a woman have been charged with holding a person in slavery or servitude after a raid by human trafficking police.
Emmanuel Edet, 59, and Antan Edet, 56, were are due to appear in court today accused of holding someone in servitude. They are also accused of assisting unlawful immigration.
They were charged yesterday after being arrested by the Met's human trafficking unit.
Both were remanded in custody to appear at Hendon Magistrates' Court on Friday.
