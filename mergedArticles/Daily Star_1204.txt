BRITISH firms should "try one of our workers" before hiring immigrants, Iain Duncan Smith said last night.
The Work and Pensions Secretary said he was surprised by the number of foreigners used to build the London 2012 stadiums.
But he was encouraged that now more jobs seem to go to Brits.
