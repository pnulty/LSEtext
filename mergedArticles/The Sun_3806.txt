OVER 130 illegal immigrants a month are being deported after tip-offs from the public, The Sun on Sunday can reveal.
Figures show 1,585 people were booted out last year after reports from locals.
Home Secretary Theresa May praised vigilant citizens as the "eyes and ears" of enforcement and urged Brits to keep up the good work.
Tory MP Charlie Elphicke added: "Britain is a welcoming country, but people here illegally should be deported."
