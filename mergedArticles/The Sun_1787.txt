UKIP boss Nigel Farage dropped two Euro election gaffes yesterday — over his foreign wife and a fake voter. He was stumped when asked why employing his German missus Kirsten was not depriving a Brit of a job — a key Ukip anti-immigration message.
Mr Farage could only insist: "I don't know anybody who would work those hours." Later it emerged Lizzy Vaid, right, a "voter" from Devon in Ukip's manifesto, is actually a party official.
