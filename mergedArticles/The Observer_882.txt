Three new titles to read
The Circle
Dave Eggers (Penguin pounds 8.99)
Prescient, highly enjoyable and deeply disconcerting satire about the tyranny of total techno-intrusion in our lives.
Almost English
Charlotte Mendelson (Picador pounds 7.99)
Funny and observant novel of family life among Hungarian immigrants, against the backdrop of a British boarding school.
The Democracy Project
David Graeber (Penguin pounds 9.99)
Compelling and sympathetic account of the Occupy movement and why, in the author's view, it succeeded.
