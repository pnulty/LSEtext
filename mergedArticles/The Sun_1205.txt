A NATIONAL action plan to combat bigots was called for yesterday on International Day Against Racism.
There have been 58 reported racist incidents since the start of 2014.
It compares to 20 in the same period last year.
Immigrant Council of Ireland chief Denise Charlton called for the new plan "on the basis of these figures and testimonies of people who have come forward to us".
