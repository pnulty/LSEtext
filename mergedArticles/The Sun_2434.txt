MANDATORY training should be given to airline crews and airport staff to spot potential victims of trafficking, according to the Immigrant Council.
New figures released by Justice Minister Alan Shatter showed gardai identified 106 suspected victims of trafficking for forced labour since 2008, including four already this year.
Nusha Yonkova, coordinator with the Immigrant Council of Ireland, said: "It is clear trafficking of human beings is our biggest crime — bigger even than the drug trade."
