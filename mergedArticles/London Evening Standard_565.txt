in brief
EASTERN European beggars have defied a crackdown and returned to a makeshift camp in Park Lane.
Police and immigration officials swooped last week to arrest a gang of 24 rough-sleepers after tourists and business owners complained, and ordered them to return to their home country. But a group of them were back last night, with belongings in plastic carrier bags.
