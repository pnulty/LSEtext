COALITION MPs faced embarrassing questions about their cleaners and children's nannies in a row about the immigration boom being fuelled by the "wealthy metropolitan elite" employing foreigners. Lib Dem MP Jeremy Browne, who was sacked as a Home Office minister last year, pleaded not guilty to the charge. "I do my own cleaning. I don't earn enough money to afford any domestic staff," he told the BBC Sunday Politics show ruefully.
