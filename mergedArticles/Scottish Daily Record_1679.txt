DEL Amitri's Justin Currie had to disappoint US fans after failing to get his visa in time for his American tour.
He told fans: "Due to unforeseen immigration/visa issues I am sadly forced to reschedule my entire US tour.
"The dates are now being rebooked for September 2014. I have never cancelled a single show before so I am extremely disappointed and embarrassed."
