A STUDENT labelled Ukip's 'ethnic minority poster girl' has quit the party, accusing leaders of 'racist populism'. British Asian Sanya-Jeet Thandi, 21, added its campaign on immigration had turned next week's Euro elections into 'a game of us and them'. Ukip was 'exploiting ignorance,' she added.
