BRITAIN Briefing
CONSERVATIVE election hopes are being jeopardised by the party's "relentless focus" on immigration, a Tory backbencher warned as he issued an appeal to David Cameron to stop trying to "out-Ukip Ukip."
Mark Field suggested that the Prime Minister's "undeliverable" pledge to cut net migration to the tens of thousands was helping fuel a public perception that his party was "fundamentally hostile" to immigrants.
