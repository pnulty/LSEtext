Three Pounds in My Pocket
11AM RADIO 4
How in the Fifties, thousands of immigrants came to Britain from the Indian subcontinent, many arriving with no more than £3 in their pocket - the limit set by the Indian authorities.
Ravel at Wigmore Hall
12NOON RADIO 3
Radio 3 clears its schedule today to explore one of the most successful French composers - Maurice Ravel, creator of Bolero - and the day begins with the Nash Ensemble performing from Ravel's repertoire.
