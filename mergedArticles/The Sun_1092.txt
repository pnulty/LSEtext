THE taxpayer footed a '545,000 airfare bill to fly 209 illegal immigrants out of the country last year, it has emerged.
Figures also revealed that 82 deportees were sent home on specially chartered flights at an average cost of more than '3,700 per person.
Justice officials insisted it was cheaper than keeping them in Ireland. Some 2,200 people were deported or turned back at entry ports in 2013.
