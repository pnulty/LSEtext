WAR OF THE WORLDS
Sunday, 9pm, Channel 5
Typical, isn't it? You're a divorced dad trying to build up a relationship with your kids then a bunch of pesky aliens decide to invade earth and liquidise your neighbours. However, it turns out to be a blessing in disguise for Tom Cruise, as he gets to know his offspring while trying to get them to safety.
The SOCIAL NETWORK
Sunday, 10pm, Channel 4
How could they make a movie about Facebook? Quite easily, from the evidence of this superb drama. Computer nerd Mark Zuckerberg is asked to design a social networking website for millionaire brothers. But when he creates his own similar website, they sue.
CHARLIE AND THE CHOCOLATE FACTORY
Monday, 6pm, Channel 5
Johnny Depp teams up with director Tim Burton for a remake of this classic book and film. Penniless Charlie Bucket is one of five children to win a tour of Willy Wonka's magical sweet factory. Great fun with some excellent special effects.
HARRY POTTER AND THE GOBLET OF FIRE
Tuesday, 7.30pm, ITV
Harry is chosen to represent Hogwarts in the magical Triwizard Tournament. Meanwhile, the followers of Lord Voldemort plot to restore him to the land of the living. The special effects are ramped up for one of the best films of the franchise.
ONCE
Wednesday, 11.50pm, BBC1
In this quirky little film, a Dublin vacuum-cleaner repairman and an immigrant Czech pianist discover they're kindred spirits when it comes to music. So they set out to record an album together in this unusual romantic musical drama.
